u} y executes  u y to calculate the item indexes m to be used at each atom, and then produces the result  m} y .

---

### Common Uses

The construct u} y is an obsolete relic of early J, superseded by the gerund form of [Composite Items](https://code.jsoftware.com/wiki/Vocabulary/curlyrt "Vocabulary/curlyrt") (m} y).

[Rank Infinity](https://code.jsoftware.com/wiki/Vocabulary/RankInfo#rank_~ "Vocabulary/RankInfo") _\-- operates on \[x and\] y as a whole --_ [WHY IS THIS IMPORTANT?](https://code.jsoftware.com/wiki/Vocabulary/RankInfoIsImportant "Vocabulary/RankInfoIsImportant")

---

x u} y executes  x u y to calculate the _atom numbers_ to be modified, and then replaces those atoms of y with atoms of x.

The **_atom number_** of the atom of y whose index list is d is  ($y)#.d .

---

### Common uses

The construct  x u} y is an obsolete relic of early J, superseded by the gerund form of [Amend](https://code.jsoftware.com/wiki/Vocabulary/curlyrt#dyadic "Vocabulary/curlyrt") (x m} y).
